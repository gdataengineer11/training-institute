We process your data to perform a service you request and, where applicable, with your consent (for certain notifications). In India, we follow the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023. If you are in the EU/EEA/UK, our legal bases include contract and legitimate interests, and—where required—consent.

5) Sharing & international transfers

We do not sell your personal data. We share it only with service providers we use to run the service, for example:

  • Meta/WhatsApp Cloud API: to send/receive messages and status updates.
  • Hosting & automation providers: e.g., our n8n server/hosting, database, secure storage, and email service.

Where data is transferred internationally, we rely on appropriate safeguards (such as standard contractual clauses) as provided by Meta and our processors.

6) Retention

We retain WhatsApp conversations and related logs only as long as needed to provide the service, comply with legal obligations, or resolve disputes. Media shared via WhatsApp may be stored temporarily by WhatsApp and our systems for delivery and reliability.

7) Your choices & rights

8) Security

We use technical and organizational measures (least-privilege access, encryption in transit, audit logging) to protect your data. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we work to continually enhance our safeguards.
